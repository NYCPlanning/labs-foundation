#### Stacked button groups Make a button group vertical with the `.stacked` class. The responsive `.stacked-for-small` class makes the group vertical on small screens only, and `.stacked-for-medium` makes the group vertical on medium and small screens. These classes are good for button groups confined to small spaces at certain screen sizes. ```
