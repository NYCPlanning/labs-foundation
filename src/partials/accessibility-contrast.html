{{{headerify 'h3' 'Contrast'}}} {{#markdown}} For users with low vision, proper typographic contrast is important. Both size and color matter. Text colors should stand out from background colors. Use the [Color Contrast Checker ](https://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/) to choose accessible colors. The contrast ratio should at least be 1:4.5 for normal text and 3:1 for large text. {{/markdown}}
