{{{headerify 'h3' 'Color basics'}}} {{#markdown}} Promote strong brand recognition by using a distinct and reduced color palette, featuring only two hues (orange and yellow) and subtle shades of gray. The primary brand color is dcp-orange . It's the color of the "NYC" in our logo, and it can be used for large blocks of color. The slightly darker a11y-orange increases accessibility and should be used for buttons and linked text. The secondary brand color is dcp-yellow . Use it in small amounts as an accent and for secondary UI elements. The lighter a11y-yellow increases accessibility and can be used for linked text on darker backgrounds. - **Orange implies clickability** in our products. Do not use orange to highlight or emphasize words. - Do not overuse color. Color should not cover the majority of the page. The default background color of the `` element is off-white . light, visual hierarchy - Be sure to [check the contrast](https://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/) when choosing colors. {{/markdown}}
