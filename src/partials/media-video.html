{{{headerify 'h3' 'Video'}}} {{#markdown}} **Make sure text is legible** — When displaying text in videos, make sure that it's reasonably large, uses high-contrast colours, and remains on screen for long enough to be read. **Provide subtitles** — A lot of video is watched without sound (as much as 85 percent on Facebook). This also makes the vides accessibility for users with auditory disabilities. **Disable autoplay** — Specific sites like Facebook and YouTube play video automatically. When embedding video on the Council website, do not enable autoplay. {{/markdown}}
